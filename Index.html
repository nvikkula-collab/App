Phase guide
Daily practices
🫁
Morning breathwork (10 min)
Vagus nerve activation · 4 counts in · 7 hold · 8 out. Activates rest-and-digest mode before your first juice.
🖐️
Dry brushing (5 min)
Lymphatic drainage · brush toward the heart · start at feet · activates toxin clearance pathways.
🚶
Gentle movement (20–30 min)
Walk, gentle yoga, rebounding. No intense exercise — your body is doing deep work. Support it, do not exhaust it.
🌙
Evening wind-down (10 min)
Body scan, gratitude, tomorrow's intention. Write three things your body did well today.
Signs it is working
✨
Skin clarity improving
Inflammation leaving the body shows first in the skin. A temporary breakout on days 3–7 is normal — toxins leaving.
⚡
Energy shifting around day 7–10
The initial fatigue gives way to a cleaner, steadier energy. No spikes, no crashes. Your body is finding balance.
😴
Sleep deepening
As gut inflammation reduces, the gut-brain axis calms. Better gut = better sleep. Most notice this around day 10–14.
🎯
Sugar cravings dropping
The gut bacteria that demand sugar are being starved. Most people notice cravings shifting dramatically around day 10–14.
When things feel hard
🤕
Headache days 1–4
Normal detox response. Water, rest, gentle movement. Caffeine withdrawal peaks day 2–3. This passes.
😤
Emotional waves
The gut holds emotions. As it releases, you may feel unexpected sadness, anger, or tears. This is healing, not breakdown.
🍕
Intense cravings
Peak at days 3–5, then dramatically reduce. Drink an extra juice, go for a walk, use the emergency protocol.
I see you. Stay with me. 🌿
You are not quitting. You are having a hard moment. There is a difference. Here is what to do right now.
1 — Stop and breathe (2 minutes)
Place one hand on your belly. Breathe in for 4 counts. Hold for 7. Out for 8. Do this 4 times. Your nervous system is activated — this resets it.
2 — Remember why you started
Close your eyes. Picture yourself on day 40. The bloating gone. The energy you felt in the first week. The version of you that made it. She is real. She is waiting.
3 — Write one sentence
"I am staying because _______." Fill it in. Write it down. Say it out loud. The act of naming your reason restores your commitment.
4 — Drink a juice right now
Cravings are often dehydration or low blood sugar. A celery or green juice in the next 10 minutes changes the body chemistry of this moment.
5 — Tell the community
"Hard moment, day X. I almost quit. I didn't." Post it. Someone will respond within minutes. You are not alone in this.
